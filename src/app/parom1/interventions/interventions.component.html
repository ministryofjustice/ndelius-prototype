Interventions

  • All interventions completed throughout the sentence - not just those finished since the last report was submitted. Record these chronologically with dates. For example, programmes, courses, 121 work, education, training, OPD intervention services employment and progression regimes.
  • Referrals and programme assessments - state if the prisoner was not deemed suitable.
  • Interventions the prisoner refused to attend or did not complete.
  • A summary of the impact that an intervention has had on the prisoner, or the lack of impact. You do not have to analyse interventions individually - this is included in the dossier in reports like the post-sentence or Structured Assessment of Risk (SARN).
  • Achievements, shifts in behaviour or attitude, responsivity and diversity considerations (for example IQ, learning disabilities, personality disorder or maturity) and capacity to change.
  • Application of learning, for example, evidence of using a new skill.
  • A summary of the impact of gender-specific and trauma-informed interventions.
