Oral hearing

  • An explanation of the prisoner’s ability to effectively engage at the oral hearing so that appropriate provisions can be made.
  • Consideration of the prisoner’s health, diversity or security information. For example, there is a working hearing loop or BSL if the prisoner is deaf or hard of hearing, there is an interpreter or intermediary if English is a second language or there is sufficient staff if the prisoner is on 6 man unlock.
  • A statement confirming the necessary arrangements have been made.
  • A statement confirming oral hearing considerations have been addressed if there are no issues.
